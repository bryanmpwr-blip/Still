# Privacy Policy — noDisturb

**Last updated: June 24, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

noDisturb ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Name / Username** — Your display name, used to personalize your in-app profile.
- **Email Address** — Used to create and authenticate your account via our backend service (Supabase).
- **User ID** — A unique identifier assigned to your account to maintain your data across sessions.

### Screen Time Data
noDisturb uses Apple's Screen Time API (DeviceActivity framework) to help you manage your app usage. **This data never leaves your device.** Screen time statistics are stored locally using Apple's secure on-device storage and are never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:
- Create and manage your account
- Authenticate you securely
- Display your profile within the app
- Sync your preferences and settings across devices

We do **not** use your data for advertising, marketing, or analytics purposes.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

Your account data (name, email, user ID) is stored securely using **Supabase**, a SOC 2 Type II compliant backend platform. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Screen time data is processed entirely on-device using Apple's DeviceActivity framework and is never transmitted externally.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use **Supabase** (supabase.com) as our backend infrastructure for authentication and profile storage. Supabase's privacy policy is available at [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

We do **not** integrate any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or data brokers.

---

## 6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.
